Why Nexus Exists

"How do I protect my community without breaking the bank?"

After watching servers get raided and seeing security bots charge $10/month for basic protection, I realized something was broken. Premium features shouldn't be locked behind paywalls when your community's safety is at stake.

So I built Nexus - AI-powered threat detection, automatic recovery, and hourly snapshots. Everything a server needs, without the subscription fee.

Why open source? When a bot has admin permissions, you should be able to see exactly what it does with them. Transparency matters.
