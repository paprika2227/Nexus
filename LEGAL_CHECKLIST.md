## Important Notes

### Privacy Policy
- Must be accessible
- Must explain data collection
- Must explain data usage
- Must explain user rights

### Terms of Service
- Must be clear and readable
- Must explain acceptable use
- Must include liability limitations
- Must include termination clauses

### Data Handling
- Must be transparent
- Must explain retention
- Must explain sharing
- Must explain security
